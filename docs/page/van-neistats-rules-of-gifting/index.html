Resources
  1. All gifts should contain an element of surprise.
    • If they know what they're getting, surprise them with when or how you give it to them.
  2. Respect the gifting Holy Trinity:
    1. Nice
    2. Made
    3. Thoughtful
    • Any two will be a good gift
  3. Made gifts should not be bigger than one's hand.
  4. Dating and/or monograming a gift gives it extra power.
  5. Know what flowers your girl likes.
  6. Take your time with books.
    • Get a signed copy if it's available.
    • Date and write your name in one of the leaves of the book.
  7. A postcard COUNTS!
    • Works particularly for birthdays.
    1. Don't brag about where you are.
    2. Make it about them.
    3. Put a joke or some kind of line in there.
  8. TWO DAYS.
    1. Day to rack your brain, sleep on it.
    2. Get the epiphany, execute the gift.
    • Does not have to be two consecutive days.
  9. She will tell you.
    • Take notes.
    • Be precise.
    • NOT if you ask.
  10. Buy small gifts when you travel.
    • Stockpile them.
    • Even if you don't know who they're for.
  11. Expensive, not costly.
  12. For KIDS: You pick the store, the kid picks the gift.
    • For when you are buying a present that a kid is going to be giving to someone else.
  13. For gift RECEIVERS: Send a thank you note in the mail.
GOAL:
  • Make the receiver cry.
  • Make people feel loved.
    • (Even if you don't really love them.)
